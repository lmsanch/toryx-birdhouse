# Agent Teams

Working with multi-agent teams in Birdhouse. Agent teams allow multiple AI
agents to collaborate on tasks, communicating with each other and working
together.

## Creating a Team

Set up a team of agents that can work together on your project.

## Team Communication

Agents in a team can actively communicate and collaborate, sharing context and
coordinating their work.

## Managing Teams

Monitor and steer your agent teams as they work.
