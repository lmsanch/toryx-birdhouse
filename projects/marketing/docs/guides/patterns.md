# Patterns

Saving and reusing workflow patterns in Birdhouse. Patterns let you turn your
favourite workflows into reusable templates.

## Saving a Pattern

Found a workflow that works? Save it as a pattern to reuse it later with natural
language.

## Using Patterns

Invoke saved patterns by describing what you want in natural language. Birdhouse
will match your request to the right pattern.

## Built-in Patterns

Birdhouse includes several useful built-in patterns to get you started.
